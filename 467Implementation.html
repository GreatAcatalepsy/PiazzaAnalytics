Piazza Linguistic Analysis for Graphical Examination



What is PLAGE?

tl;dr: An analytic tool for professors and TAs to use to analyze a Piazza forum after the conclusion of a class

Piazza is an incredibly useful class tool which bolsters a forum as well as relevant resources and analytical features. However, the analytics are difficult to understand. It is very difficult to glean information about the class from them short of just some basic information such as "who were top posters?" and "how many posts were there?" Therefore, our group sought to improve the existing analytics. By doing so, it is our hope that professors can better analyze the dynamics of a class, including but not limited to awssignments that were particularly notable and discussion worthy as well as take a good look at traffic as well as tone of the discussion and popularity of certain discussions
